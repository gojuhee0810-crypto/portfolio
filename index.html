About my story

I design digital experiences where interaction is treated as data and system as structure. I specialize in UX and funnel optimization, creating user-centered solutions ranging from well-thought-out interfaces to bold experimental mechanics.

My mission is to be a reliable partner, creating products that not only effectively solve problems but also inspire and delight. I integrate business impact and user-centric research to build solid frameworks.

Career Highlights

Recently working at Kakao Pay (Ad Growth / Marketing Content) and previously at Project Rent, I have actively shaped product strategies. From implementing gamification UI that achieved 71% CTR to redesigning onboarding funnels, data-driven decisions guide my process.

With an end-to-end design approach spanning online interfaces to offline brand experiences, I believe that relentless testing and a deep understanding of user journeys can take products to heights you never imagined possible.
